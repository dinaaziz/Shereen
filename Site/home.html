Shereen Atef
Assistant Professor, Faculty of Medicine, Ainshams University

Graduated 1995, from faculty of medicine, Ain Shams University, Egypt, since that date I joined the clinical pathology department till now, promoted from assistant lecturer to lecturer and finally assistant professor . In 2008 I finished my Diploma in Total Quality Managment for Helthcare Reform (TQM) from American University in Cairo. I have special interest in molecular biology, choosing my MD thesis on molecular marker of bladder cancer and teaching molecular biology course to under and postgraduated students. I have several international publications in American medical journals as (Journal of Brain and development and Journal of Neonatal perinatal medicine) in addition to many regional publications. Since 2008 I am working as laboratory director and infection control director in Alsalama Hospital, Abu Dhabi, UAE.
